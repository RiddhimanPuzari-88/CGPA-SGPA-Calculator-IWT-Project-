Notice
  • Curriculum Scope: Default subjects and credit values are based on the KU CSE Curriculum. If your batch or department has different subjects, subject changes can be made by the developer. Contact him through the "Contact Developer" section.
  • Grading System: Marks are converted to grades as follows — O (90–100), A+ (80–89), A (70–79), B+ (60–69), B (50–59), C (40–49), F (below 40). Grade points: O=10, A+=9, A=8, B+=7, B=6, C=5, F=0.
  • For Reference Only: This calculator is an unofficial tool meant to help students estimate their SGPA/CGPA. Always verify results with your official university records.
